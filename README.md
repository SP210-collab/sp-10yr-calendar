# SP's 10 Year Calendar

A subscribable, rolling 2-year `.ics` feed layered over a 20-year Auckland/NZ rhythm database.

Scores every day for:
- **Noise** (1 = clean, 5 = noisy)
- **Outreach** — best days for B2B targeting
- **Billing** — best days for AR / invoicing
- **Deep Work** — best days for focus
- **Family** — best days for personal time

## Subscribe

Paste any of these URLs into Google Calendar / Outlook Online / Apple Calendar:

```
https://sp210-collab.github.io/sp-10yr-calendar/sp_10yr_daily.ics
https://sp210-collab.github.io/sp-10yr-calendar/sp_10yr_highlights.ics
https://sp210-collab.github.io/sp-10yr-calendar/sp_10yr_all.ics
```

- **daily** (recommended) — one all-day marker per day showing the noise rating + opportunity scores + top drivers
- **highlights** — only days with N≥4 events
- **all** — full event list

## Browse

[cycles_preview.html](./cycles_preview.html) — monthly opportunity calendar, best-days-per-purpose lists, full event tables, weekly patterns.

## Coverage

Events derived from:
- NZ public holidays (Holidays Act 2003 + Matariki Act 2022)
- IRD (PAYE/GST/FBT/provisional/terminal tax)
- MSD (benefit paydays, NZ Super)
- Auckland Council (rates)
- MoE (school terms)
- RBNZ (OCR decisions)
- Courts NZ (summer recess)
- REINZ (property seasons)
- Tourism (cruise season, festivals, marathons)
- Cultural (Chinese, Indian, Islamic, Pacific calendars)
- Traffic (AT/NZTA roadworks, long-weekend exodus)
- Private paydays (weekly Thu/Fri, fortnightly Thu)
- Rent cycles (weekly/monthly)
- Business cycles (EOM invoicing, 20th payment, EOQ push, FY planning)
- Family (school drop-off/pickup, kids' sports)
- Weather (cyclone/winter/flu seasons)
- **Australia** (federal holidays, AU FY, RBA, NSW school hols, Melbourne Cup)
- **USA** (federal holidays, FOMC, Tax Day, Thanksgiving, earnings)

AU/US events are scored for *NZ-side operational impact*, not local impact.
