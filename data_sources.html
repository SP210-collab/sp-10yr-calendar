Bias guardrails

The following were explicitly excluded from rule-based encoding on the grounds that they should be measured, not assumed:
  • Demographic persona effects (age / gender / ethnicity as drivers of city rhythm)
  • "Masses moving together" psychology beyond explicit money-flow mechanics
  • Anecdotal traffic/foot-traffic claims without Stats NZ or AT open-data backing
The dashboard should surface these patterns when live data reveals them — never via hardcoded assumption.
